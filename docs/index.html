Capabilities

Built for the Field & the Office

Two role-based interfaces designed for how your teams actually work.

📷

QR Scan & Camera

Instant barcode recognition with manual fallback. Attach inspection photos with automatic compression to under 500KB.

📦

Receiving Wizard

Six-step guided flow: material details, PO/delivery, inspection photos, condition assessment, accept/reject decision, location assignment.

📍

Yard Management

Define zones, racks, and positions. Assign materials to specific locations with searchable inventory browse and filters.

Exception Handling

Flagged items for damage, wrong type, or count mismatch flow into a dedicated exception queue for office resolution.

📈

Dashboard & Reports

KPI cards, inventory charts, yard overview, aging reports, and CSV export — all from the office tab group.

Offline-First

Full write queue and read cache. Field workers stay productive with zero connectivity — data syncs automatically when back online.
