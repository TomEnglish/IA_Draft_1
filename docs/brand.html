Invenio marks & usage

All brand assets live under /brand/. Four SVGs cover every placement: primary mark (light), primary mark (dark), horizontal lockup, and favicon. Every file is pure SVG — infinitely scalable, text-selectable, theme-aware.

01 — Primary mark

Mark on light + dark

Two variants. Light mark uses the sky-700 → cyan-600 gradient with a white I. Dark mark brightens the gradient (cyan-400 → cyan-600) and flips the I to canvas-dark for contrast on dark surfaces. Use the correct variant for the background — never recolor the art.

Size ladder

04 — Clearspace

Safe margin

Keep at least 1× the mark's height of clear space on every side. Inside that zone, no other text, icons, or graphic elements. The dashed inner box shows safe-area; the outer box shows total reserved space.

07 — Usage

Do and don't

Consistency beats creativity with brand marks. These rules cover 99% of the bad-usage patterns we'd see.

Do
Use the exact SVG as shipped.
Link directly to /brand/invenio-mark.svg. Don't re-export or rasterize unless you must hit a PNG-only surface.
Do
Swap light ↔ dark variant based on background.
Light mark on light surfaces; dark mark on dark surfaces. See dark theme tokens.
Do
Respect clearspace (1× mark height).
Especially in page headers and email signatures where things get crowded.
Don't
Recolor the gradient.
No off-brand blues, no monochrome flattening, no rainbow pride-month variants unless formally approved.
Don't
Skew, rotate, or stretch.
The mark is a square. Scale uniformly — never disproportionately.
Don't
Place on a busy photo background.
Put it on a solid-color panel if the page background is noisy. Contrast matters.
